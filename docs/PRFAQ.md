# TaxLens — Understand Your Tax History, Privately

**For immediate release**

Today we're announcing TaxLens, a web app that lets individuals visualize and chat with their tax return history — without ever giving up their data.

Most people file their taxes every year and immediately forget what happened. They have no idea how their effective rate has changed, why they owed more last year, or whether they're leaving deductions on the table. The tools that exist are either filing software (TurboTax, FreeTaxUSA) that helps you file but not understand, or enterprise products built for accountants — not individuals.

TaxLens connects to your Google Drive, reads your tax return PDFs directly in your browser, and uses AI to extract and visualize your history across years. Charts, trends, year-over-year comparisons. Then you can chat with it: *"Why did I owe so much in 2022?"* or *"Am I in a good position to sell stock this year?"*

The privacy story is the product: **your tax documents never touch our servers.** They go from your Google Drive to Claude for parsing, and the structured data gets saved back to your own Drive. We store nothing.

## FAQ

**Who is this for?**
Anyone who files a US 1040 and wants to actually understand their tax situation — not just file and forget. Especially useful for people with multiple income sources: RSUs, brokerage accounts, freelance income, or foreign income (India, etc.).

**How is this different from TurboTax?**
TurboTax helps you file. TaxLens helps you understand what you filed — across all your years, in one place, with an AI you can ask questions. It works with returns filed anywhere, not just TurboTax.

**How does the privacy model work?**
Your PDFs are fetched directly in your browser from Google Drive, sent to Claude for parsing, and the result is saved back to your Drive. Our server never receives or stores your tax documents or parsed data. We use Google's narrowest Drive scope — we can only access files you explicitly open through the app.

**What about people who file in India too?**
First-class support for India-US filers: Zerodha capital gains, TDS/Form 26AS, Foreign Tax Credit (Form 1116) tracking, FBAR threshold checker. This is a severely underserved group with no good tools today.

**Does it help me file?**
Not directly — it's an analysis and planning tool. But it can flag issues ("your RSU cost basis looks off"), surface opportunities ("a larger 401k contribution would drop your bracket"), and help you walk into filing season prepared.

**What does it cost?**
Free tier with limited years of history. Paid plan (~$20/year) for unlimited years, India/foreign income features, and current-year projection tools.
